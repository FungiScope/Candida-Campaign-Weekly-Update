Key facts about the FungiScope® Candida Campaign

  • Study Period: 2024–2026
  • Clinical data collection: retrospective, anonymized data entry
  • Patient population: adult patients with any invasive Candida infections diagnosed in 2024 and after
  • Follow up: 3 months for survivors
  • Clinical isolates: not required
  • Compensation: provided for valid cases
